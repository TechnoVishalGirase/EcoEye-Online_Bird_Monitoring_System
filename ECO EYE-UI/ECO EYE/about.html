Our Mission

Birds play an essential role in maintaining healthy ecosystems. They are not only a source of natural beauty and inspiration but also act as pollinators, pest controllers, and indicators of environmental health. Despite their importance, many bird species face threats from habitat destruction, climate change, pollution, and human interference. BirdsWatch aims to combat these challenges by conducting detailed monitoring and research to inform conservation strategies, influence policy-making, and educate the public on the importance of avian conservation.ECO-EYE is more than a project; it's a movement towards safeguarding our planet's biological diversity. Whether you are a bird enthusiast, a researcher, or someone passionate about conservation, there is a place for you in our community. By monitoring bird populations, protecting habitats, and fostering a love for nature, we can make a lasting impact on the health of our planet.

Our Vision

Utilizing the latest in tracking technology and data analysis, we conduct comprehensive surveys and long-term monitoring programs to study bird populations, migration patterns, breeding success, and habitat usage.Recognizing the critical link between birds and their habitats, we work on habitat restoration and protection projects, ensuring birds have safe breeding and feeding grounds.We believe in the power of community. Through workshops, citizen science programs, and public awareness campaigns, we engage people of all ages in birdwatching activities, data collection, and conservation efforts.Collaborating with academic institutions and conservation organizations, we pioneer research on avian health, behavior, and ecology, leading to innovative conservation solutions.Armed with data and research findings, we advocate for policies and practices that protect bird species and their habitats, working closely with governmental and non-governmental organizations
